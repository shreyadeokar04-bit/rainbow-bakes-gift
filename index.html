Our Story

About Rainbow Bakes & Gift

"Every celebration deserves something handmade with love."

Our Beginning

Rainbow Bakes & Gift started as a small home kitchen in Pimpri, Pune — born from a passion for baking and a desire to make every celebration memorable. What began as baking cakes for family and friends quickly grew into a beloved local brand known for handcrafted quality and personalized service.

We believe that the best gifts come from the heart, and nothing says "I care" quite like something made by hand, just for you. Every cake, every chocolate, every hamper is crafted with attention to detail and a whole lot of love.

Made with Love

Every item is handcrafted in our home kitchen using premium ingredients. No mass production — just genuine care and attention to every detail.

Fresh & Quality

We use only the freshest ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers. No preservatives, no shortcuts — just honest, delicious baking.

Fully Customizable

From flavors to packaging to personal messages — everything can be tailored to your preferences and the occasion.

Premium Packaging

Beautiful, Instagram-worthy packaging that makes unboxing an experience. Eco-friendly options available on request.
